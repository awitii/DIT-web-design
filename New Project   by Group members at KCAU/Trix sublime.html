About Me

Awiti Darman Angila has a profound passion for videography, which serves as a powerful medium for him to express his creativity and tell compelling stories. He is captivated by the way moving images can convey emotions and narratives in a dynamic and immersive manner. For Awiti, videography is more than just capturing moments; it's about weaving together visual elements to create a cohesive and impactful story. This passion drives him to constantly explore new techniques, experiment with different styles, and push the boundaries of his craft, making his work stand out in the ever-evolving world of visual storytelling..
